About Ti'Oluwa Ni

Ti’Oluwa Ni Academy emphasizes a holistic approach to education, it combines academic excellence with character development. The school's philosophy is rooted in its "FIRST" values: Focus, Integrity, Respect, Self-Determination and Teamwork, which guide students to care for themselves and others. The curriculum is broad and balanced, designed to produce respectful, open-minded and resilient citizens who contribute positively to society.

The school also places a strong emphasis on preparing students for life in modern ways and fostering diversity and inclusion. Through various co-curricular activities, students are encouraged to develop their personal skills, cultural awareness, and a passion for lifelong learning.

Success Stories

The quest to improve students’ engagement prompted the leadership of Ti’Oluwa Ni International Academy to integrate educational technology into their classrooms. They installed 15 interactive smartboards and provided 100 tablets to students, which transformed lessons into dynamic and interactive experiences. This shift led to a 40% increase in classroom participation and a 30% improvement in exam scores, with significant gains in Mathematics and English.

The personalized learning paths enabled by the e-learning platform helped 80% of students make substantial progress with many improving their grades by 15% or more. The flexibility of online learning also reduced absenteeism by 20%. Teachers who are initially skeptical later found the technology enhanced their teaching and 90% reported increased satisfaction. Parents also noticed the positive changes with 85% expressing approval of the new learning approach.

As a result of these efforts, Ti’Oluwa Ni International Academy received recognition for "Best Innovation in Education," and graduation rates rose by 10%. The school’s success story illustrates how thoughtful integration of technology can revitalize education, transforming not only academic outcomes but also the entire school community.
